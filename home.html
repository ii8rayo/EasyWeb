About The Platform

The platform is the electronic platform of the Ministry of Interior, aimed at providing integrated digital services to citizens, residents, and visitors of Canada. This is achieved by leveraging technological capabilities and harnessing modern technologies to provide users with a distinguished user experience through automated services that are reliable, secure, and highly efficient.

The Vision

- Providing a digital platform and unified interface for the services of the ministry of interoir to be the first choice for the beneficiary, and to enhance an electronic integration and innovation smarter quality services.

THE MESSSAGE

Providing services from the sectors of the Ministry of Interior and aligning them with the targets of the countries vision and utilizing technological capabilities and Al to elevate the services of the sectors to a new innovative level that reaches every internal and external beneficiary.

The OBJECTIVES

• Building an integrated electronic system that embodies the concept of a unified gateway for the services of the Ministry of Interior and its sectors.
• Achieving digital transformation.
• Eliminating manual transactions, reengineering procedures, and enhancing. them with the capabilities of artificial intelligence.
• Improving the quality of life for citizens, residents, and visitors.
• Achieving transparency and combating corruption.

Summary

This Platform is a Canadian government e-services for citizens, residents, and visitors. Including passport and visa services, appointment scheduling, traffic violation payments, and more.
